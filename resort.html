Resorts in Maldives

Surrounded by jungle, this high-end all-inclusive resort is on a secluded tropical island in the Laccadive Sea.

The bright villas feature thatched roofs, open-air bathrooms and terraces. All include minibars, flat-screens and free Wi-Fi. Upgraded villas, some over the water, add outdoor pools and/or glass-floored living areas. Room service is available. There are 2 outdoor pools, a gym and a spa, as well as a children's play area, 7 restaurants and 6 bars. Other amenities include snorkeling, kayaking and pedal boats, as well as scuba diving and catamaran trips. Breakfast is available for a fee.
